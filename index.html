About Me

I'm a senior computer science major at the University of Central Florida with 4+ years of C++ experience. I have a passion for computer graphics, real-time rendering, and performance driven real-time software development. I enjoy low level software and building things from scratch to learn how tools and libraries are built. One of my goals is to learn what it takes to ship stable and reliable C/C++ projects. I will be going back to UCF during Fall 2025 to purse my masters degree.

My current interests are graphics APIs such as Direct3D 11, Windows platform development, software architecture, and data oriented design. Some things that I'd like to learn in the future is Vulkan, x86 Assembly, low level audio programming, and the SPIR-V shader compilation pipeline.
